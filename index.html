21+ Years • 5,000+ Cases

Jennifer Stewart was born in Puyallup, Washington, and spent much of her early life in Thurston and Lewis Counties. As a single mother in the early 1990s, she began her academic journey at Centralia Community College and ultimately earned her Associate of Arts degree.

She later attended the University of Washington, earning a Bachelor of Science degree in Speech and Hearing Sciences while working as a law clerk. Jennifer pursued licensure through the Washington State Bar Association's Rule Six Law Clerk Program and served as a Rule Nine Legal Intern in multiple jurisdictions.

After passing the Washington State Bar Exam on her first attempt in 2006, she was admitted as an attorney in 2007. Since then, she has represented thousands of clients across Washington State and is recognized as a tenacious, highly prepared, and fearless trial advocate.
